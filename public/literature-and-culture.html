Spanish Literature and Culture Programs

This directory compiles graduate programs focused on Spanish and Latin American literature and cultural studies. Programs cover periods from medieval Iberian literature through contemporary Latin American narratives, with specializations available in poetry, prose, drama, film studies, and cultural theory. Faculty research areas typically span multiple centuries and geographic regions, allowing students to develop expertise in specific literary movements, authors, or critical approaches. Each program link leads to official pages detailing course requirements, comprehensive exam structures, and dissertation expectations for doctoral candidates.
